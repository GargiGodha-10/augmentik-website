## Company Overview

Augmentik is an **AI Workforce Platform** built for staffing, recruitment, and workforce management organizations. It unifies hiring, client and vendor management, resource tracking, invoicing, and knowledge management into a single AI-augmented platform, with the stated goal of helping organizations move from manual, fragmented recruitment operations to a streamlined, intelligent, single-platform workflow.

**Augmentik**

Prateek Sanganeria is the CEO of APPCRAVE TECHNOVATIONS PRIVATE LIMITED, the company that developed and owns the Augmentik platform.

## Mission

To empower staffing and recruitment businesses with smarter execution — replacing fragmented, manual hiring processes with a unified, AI-powered platform that reduces time-to-hire and increases operational accuracy.

*(Phrased from the platform's own positioning: "Empowering Businesses with Smarter Execution.")*

## Vision

To become the AI-first operating system for staffing and workforce management companies — the single platform where recruitment, client relationships, vendor relationships, resourcing, compliance, and finance all operate together instead of across disconnected tools.

## Platform Overview

Augmentik is positioned as an **AI Workforce Platform** that helps organizations:

- Streamline hiring and recruitment operations
- Discover and match the right talent to the right requirements
- Simplify workforce management end-to-end
- Operate from one unified platform instead of multiple disconnected tools

The platform combines traditional staffing-agency operations (client management, vendor management, requirements, invoicing) with AI-driven capabilities (resume screening, job description parsing, candidate matching, and a conversational AI assistant).

## Industry

**IT Staffing & Recruitment Technology (HR Tech / Staffing SaaS)** — Augmentik serves organizations operating in the staffing and recruitment industry, with particular relevance to IT staffing firms, though the platform's modules (client management, vendor management, invoicing, resourcing) generalize to staffing operations broadly.

## Target Users

- Staffing and recruitment agencies
- IT staffing companies
- Internal talent acquisition / HR teams managing high-volume hiring
- Recruiters and recruitment leads
- Operations and account management teams handling client and vendor relationships
- Finance teams responsible for invoicing tied to placements

## Core Objectives

1. **Reduce time-to-hire** through AI-assisted candidate discovery and screening.
2. **Centralize operations** — clients, vendors, requirements, resources, invoicing, and documents in one system.
3. **Improve decision quality** by giving recruiters and leadership real-time insight into pipelines, resources, and performance.
4. **Automate repetitive work** — resume screening, job description parsing, and knowledge lookup — so human recruiters focus on relationships and judgment calls.
5. **Maintain compliance and traceability** through audit logs, released documents, and structured workflows.

## AI Capabilities

Augmentik's AI layer, powered in part by its assistant **Auggie**, includes:

- **AI JD Search Assistant** — extracts and interprets key hiring requirements from job descriptions.
- **AI Find Resources** — AI-powered candidate/resource matching against open requirements.
- **AI Resume Screener** — automated resume shortlisting based on defined screening criteria.
- **Knowledge Assistant (Auggie)** — a conversational AI assistant that answers questions using the organization's own knowledge base, offers walkthroughs, and provides instant guidance across the platform.

## Platform Highlights

- Unified dashboard covering recruitment, client/vendor relationships, resourcing, and finance.
- End-to-end recruitment workflow from sourcing through onboarding.
- Built-in document and knowledge management (Released Documents, Knowledge Docs).
- Structured audit logging for compliance and accountability.
- AI assistant embedded directly into the platform experience rather than offered as a separate bolt-on tool.
